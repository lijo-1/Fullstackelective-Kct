Welcome, Parent!

Here you can view your child’s progress and communicate with staff.

Your Dashboard

Your personalized dashboard allows you to stay updated on your child's academic journey. Easily access grades, attendance, and upcoming assignments.

Communication Center

Need to reach out to a teacher or counselor? Use our messaging system to send and receive messages directly from school staff. You can also schedule parent-teacher meetings through the platform.

Progress Reports

View detailed progress reports that highlight your child’s strengths and areas for improvement. Reports are updated regularly to reflect the most current information.

Attendance Records

Keep track of your child's attendance and punctuality. Review daily attendance logs and receive notifications if your child is absent or tardy.

School Announcements

Stay informed about important school events, announcements, and deadlines. The announcements section keeps you in the loop about what's happening in your child's school.

Support

If you have any questions or need assistance, our support team is here to help. Visit the support section for FAQs, contact information, and more.
